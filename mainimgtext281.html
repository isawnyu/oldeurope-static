Found in a domestic context, this spherical vessel rests on a hollow cylindrical stand and is covered by a bell-shaped lid. The three elements were meant to be used as an ensemble and are covered with a pattern of interlocking black spiral outlines placed against a red background. While the vessel’s ultimate function is still subject to debate, its impressive monumental size, combined with its complex tripartite construction and intricate decoration, suggests a ceremonial purpose clearly distinct from the original utilitarian function of ceramics.
